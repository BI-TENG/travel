About Relax Go Taiwan

Relax Go Taiwan was established in 1999, quickly gaining the No.1 reputation from Evaluation Highways Administration in 2015 with an astounding capital of 6 Million Euros. We provide more than 30 unique planned tours weekly, and customized itineraries for each and every individual guest and group that use Relax Go Tour. Our expert guides have more than five years of experience and their hearts filled with even more passion to bring our guests into experiencing traditional culture and special cuisine available only in Taiwan. The core idea of Relax Go Tour is to provide a safe and amazing experience for any and all of our guests.
